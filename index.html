FAQ

Can I post anonymously?

When posting, you can choose to make your post anonymous. If selected, your post will be published as “Anonymous user.”

Is the app free?

Yes. It’s free to use. Optional Premium unlocks extra features.

Which platforms are supported?

iOS and Android. See the store links above.

Which languages are supported?

English, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Thai, Vietnamese, and more.

Can I change my username later?

Yes. Go to Settings > Profile to update your name, username, and icon.

How can I report inappropriate content?

Use the ••• menu on a post and select Report. Our team reviews reports and may remove content or restrict accounts.

Can I block or mute users?

Yes. From a user profile, choose Block or Mute to control what you see.

Does FoodGram show ads?

Ads may be shown to free users. Premium members enjoy an ad‑free experience.

How do I cancel Premium?

Manage your subscription from the App Store or Google Play subscriptions page for your account.

What are the content guidelines?

Share food‑related, respectful, and lawful content. No harassment, spam, or illegal material.

How do I contact support?

Reach us via the app’s Help section or our official accounts listed below.
